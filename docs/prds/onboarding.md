🚀 The "Amazing" Experience
Here's where you can differentiate Proesphere. Think about who this client is — they're a homeowner or business owner who hired a contractor. They're probably anxious about their project. Their first touchpoint with your platform should make them feel taken care of.
The flow:

Admin side (30 seconds): Admin types client name + email + phone, selects the project, and optionally writes a short welcome note. One click — "Invite Client."
Client receives a beautiful, branded email — not from "Proesphere" but from their contractor's company (white-labeled). Subject line: "Your project dashboard is ready — [Company Name]". The email includes the contractor's logo, a warm welcome message, maybe even a real-time project thumbnail or progress snapshot as a teaser to drive the click.
Magic link authentication — no password at all. The client clicks one button and they're in. They land directly on their project dashboard already loaded with their project's current status. First-time visitors get a brief, elegant guided tour (3-4 tooltips max) highlighting what they can do: view progress photos, check schedule, approve selections, message their PM.
Future logins use magic links or passkeys — the client never manages a password. They get a "View your project" link every time, or they save a passkey on their phone. Zero cognitive load.
Bonus — the "wow" moment: Within minutes of being invited, the client also gets an SMS with a direct deep-link: "Hi [Name], [PM Name] from [Company] just set up your project portal. Tap to view your project →". One tap on their phone, they're looking at their project. That's the moment they think, "This contractor is professional."

Why this is powerful for Proesphere specifically:

The client never even knows "Proesphere" exists initially — it feels like the contractor built this for them. That makes the contractor look amazing, which is what makes them love and keep paying for your platform.
Zero-password auth removes the single biggest source of support tickets ("I forgot my password").
The SMS + email double-touch ensures near-100% activation rates.
The white-labeling turns every client invitation into a marketing moment for the contractor's brand.
